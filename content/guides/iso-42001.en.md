# ACF® Guide — ISO/IEC 42001:2023 (AI management system)

> **Status.** Preliminary ACF® ↔ ISO/IEC 42001:2023 mapping guide. This is **not legal advice** and not a certification opinion. ISO/IEC 42001 is a **voluntary standard** (not a legal obligation): no statutory deadline attaches to it. The mappings require **human review** (`requires_human_review`). Each clause's "text" field is a faithful summary, not the verbatim normative wording. Refer to the official standard, under ISO copyright.

## 1. Scope and applicability

ISO/IEC 42001:2023 sets the requirements for an **AI management system (AIMS)**: context, leadership, planning, support, operation, performance evaluation and improvement — following the high-level structure common to ISO management-system standards. For an agentic deployment, the AIMS is the organisational frame within which agents are designed, operated and audited.

> ISO/IEC 42001 is complementary to the regulations (AI Act, GDPR, DORA, NIS2): certification does not entail legal compliance, but provides a demonstrable management architecture.

## 2. The ACF® governance lens

- **P1 — Decision Sovereignty.** The AIMS roles and responsibilities (Annex A) are operationalised on the agent side by the DDAO (Delegated Decision Agent Officer) role and the mandate.
- **P2 — Doctrinal Traceability.** Internal audit and review (clause 9) draw on the audit trail (ACF-10) and the improvement loop (ACF-16).
- **P3 — Ultimate Human Control.** Operational control (clause 8) maps to the mandate (ACF-12) and the kill switch (ACF-07).
- **P4 — Proportional Governance.** The AI risk/impact assessment (clause 6) calibrates control intensity.

Dimensions chiefly engaged: **D2** (doctrine & framework), **D3** (design & technical control), **D4** (accountability & roles), **D5** (regulatory compliance), **D6** (audit & continuous improvement).

## 4. Lifecycle obligations for agentic deployments

### Pre-go-live
- Carry out the AI risk and impact assessment required by clause 6 — ACF-02, ACF-11.
- Define the agent's operational control (clause 8): mandate (ACF-12), kill switch (ACF-07), change control.
- Document the applicable Annex A roles and controls — ACF-12, ACF-15.

### Continuous
- Monitor, measure, analyse the AIMS and conduct internal audits (clause 9) — ACF-10.
- Maintain change control (clause 8) on every change to the agent.
- Spot gaps — `acf.identify-governance-gaps`.

### On-incident & review
- Feed the management review and improvement loop (clause 9, clause 10) — ACF-16.
- Reconstruct the agentic decision from the audit trail (ACF-10).
